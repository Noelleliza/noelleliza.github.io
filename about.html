My Background

Art and Design have always been a lifelong love of mine though my career didn’t start until 2011 when I graduated with a BFA from ‘Parsons School of Art and Design’ and began as a fashion and graphic designer in New York City.

It didn’t take long for me to discover a growing passion for communication design, and so, started self-teaching print and digital design as well as taking a continuing education course at New York’s ‘School of Visual Arts’ while still working full-time in Fashion Design.

In 2016 I realized my true passion for digital design and decided to study UX/UI Design and Frontend Development full time in order to embark on this new career path.
